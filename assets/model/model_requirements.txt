Speaker Embedding Model (1.5s) Requirements
---------------------------------------------
Sample rate: 16000 Hz (configured SAMPLE_RATE)
Window duration: 1.5 seconds
Window length (samples): 24000
Input tensor shape: (num_frames=92, num_mfcc=64) float32 (precomputed MFCCs)
MFCC extraction: librosa.feature.mfcc (n_fft=512, hop_length=256, n_mfcc=64)
Embedding dimension: 128 (dense layer 'embedding')
Training batch size: 32
Training epochs: 10
Voice profile speakers: 10
Segments per profile: 15 (1.5s each)
Demo segments: 30 (stitched 1.5s windows)
Cosine similarity: used to match stream embeddings to voice profiles
Cosine confidence threshold: COSINE_MIN_CONFIDENCE from config (segments below this may be UNKNOWN)
Output tensor shape (TFLite): (1, 128) float32 embedding vector.
